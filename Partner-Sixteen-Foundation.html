Sixteen Foundation

Introduction to Sixteen Foundation

Sixteen Foundation is a Community Organisation run by selfless volunteers. Our vision is to see communities around the world feel empowered through education. Our support ranges from the most deprived, war-torn villages to established organisations. Our mission is to enhance existing establishments to provide high quality education for each and every child.

We simply believe that an educated community is one full of optimism for its future. Our dedication towards supporting educational causes lies in the legacy we would like to uphold for our late parents. With your relentless fundraising and donations we allow a child in education, dare to dream.

Our Work Together

Brightemy, as a leading educational institute, and Sixteen Foundation, as a charitable organisation, work hand in hand to create meaningful impact in both learning and social development. Together, we focus on building opportunities for underprivileged communities, ensuring access to quality education, and promoting lifelong learning. Our joint initiatives include providing resources, training, and guidance that empower individuals to grow academically and personally. By combining Brightemy’s expertise in education with Sixteen Foundation’s dedication to service, we aim to bridge gaps in society and support sustainable growth. This collaboration not only strengthens communities but also inspires positive change through knowledge and compassion.
